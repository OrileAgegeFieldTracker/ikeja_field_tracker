Confirm Import

📋 Daily Paste — Log Today's Field Activity

Paste your daily table (Supervisor / Technical Gang / Locations) copied straight from Word or Excel. The app will match each location to a feeder from the master list automatically, and let you fix anything before saving.

Confirm Today's Field Logs

For each location, the best feeder match is pre-selected. Amber rows need your attention — either no confident match was found, or there's more than one candidate. Use the dropdown to confirm or correct each one. Nothing is saved until you click Confirm.
